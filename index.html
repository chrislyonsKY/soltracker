SolTracker v1.0.0

3D Mars rover tracking — all missions on a photorealistic globe with sol-by-sol animation.

12 missions: Perseverance, Curiosity, Opportunity, Spirit, Zhurong, Sojourner, Ingenuity, InSight, Phoenix, Viking 1&2, Beagle 2, Mars 3

Built with ArcGIS JS SDK 5.0 + Calcite. Data: NASA/JPL, CNSA, ESA, USGS, Esri.
